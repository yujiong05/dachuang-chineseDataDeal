Guiyang Industrial Expo exhibits latest technologies, products

( english.guiyang.gov.cn)

Updated: 2021-09-27

The second Guiyang Industrial Expo showcases Guizhou's latest industrial technologies and products. [Photo/colorful Guizhou network]

The second Guiyang Industrial Expo showcases Guizhou's latest industrial technologies and products. [Photo/colorful Guizhou network]

The second Guiyang Industrial Expo is being held at the Guiyang International Conference & Exhibition Center in Southwest China's Guizhou province on Sept 26-28.

This year's expo has four exhibition halls and invited more than 500 exhibitors to showcase Guizhou's latest industrial technologies, products, and achievements.

The first exhibition hall has four separate areas for new-model industrialization achievements, industrial scientific and technological achievements, achievements of mass entrepreneurship and innovation competitions for small and medium-sized enterprises, and achievements of industrial design competitions.

The China Aerospace Science and Industry Corporation showcases various vehicles, aircraft, and machinery at the expo. [Photo/colorful Guizhou network]

The China Aerospace Science and Industry Corporation showcases various vehicles, aircraft, and machinery at the expo. [Photo/colorful Guizhou network]

The second exhibition hall includes a new-energy vehicle exhibition area, advanced equipment exhibition area, and robot competition exhibition area.

The third exhibition hall focuses on the new building materials, basic materials, modern chemistry, and big data electronic information industries. It features 160 exhibition booths, with the exhibition area for new functional materials being the largest one with the widest variety of exhibits.

The fourth exhibition hall has four exhibition areas for alcoholic beverages and cigarettes, medicine, featured food, and folk crafts. Around 300 enterprises brought products to introduce Guizhou's related industries.

Guizhou's tea products are exhibited at the expo. [Photo/Guiyang news network]
