5th World Intelligence Congress kicks off in Tianjin

The fifth World Intelligence Congress has opened in Tianjin on Thursday and will run till May 23.

Global leading experts and artificial intelligence industrial leaders brainstormed at a grand summit during the opening ceremony on Thursday.

More than 30 parallel forums are being held from Thursday to Sunday to push the city's intelligent manufacturing industries to new heights.

The congress is expected to see 200 investment projects worth 100 billion yuan ($15.6 billion) ink their agreements thanks to the city's competitive edge in the intelligent manufacturing sector.

Grand exhibitions and competitions are underway in the city.

AI robots, 5G and digital technological achievements have astonished visitors at the exhibition venues.
