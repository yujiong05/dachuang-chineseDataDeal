Mahbubani: The US should cooperate with China, not contain it

At the Boao Forum for Asia Annual Conference 2025, Kishore Mahbubani, distinguished fellow of Asia Research Institute of National University of Singapore, told MediaUnlocked that China-US contest will undoubtedly intensify in the coming years. He noted that the United States is only united on one thing that the US must stop China. However, he believes that the wiser choice for the US would be to work with China.
