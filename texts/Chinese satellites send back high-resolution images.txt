标题: Chinese satellites send back high-resolution images

Chinese satellites send back high-resolution images

The SuperView-1 01 and 02 satellites are launched from Taiyuan Satellite Launch Center in Shanxi province on December 28, 2016. SuperView-1 is the first Chinese-made 0.5-meter resolution satellite and a group of high resolution pictures were sent back to earth on Tuesday.[Photo from Weichat account China Aerospace Science and Technology Corporation]
