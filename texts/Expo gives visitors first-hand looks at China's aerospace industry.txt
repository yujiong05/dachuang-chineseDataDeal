Expo gives visitors first-hand looks at China's aerospace industry

An expo displaying China's aerospace industry was held in Hefei of Anhui province on Monday with exhibits including Chang'e-5 return vehicle; samples from Lunar; and models of a carrier rocket, space suit and Tianwen 1.
