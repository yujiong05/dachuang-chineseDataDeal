Commemorative banknotes to mark aerospace achievements

Updated: 2015-11-27 15:55

(Xinhua)

Photo taken on Nov 26, 2015 shows commemorative money at a bank in the district ofDaxing'an Mountain, Northeast China's Heilongjiang province. The People's Bank of China issued a commemorative bank note and coin on Nov 26. The items, including a 100-yuan bank note and a 10-yuan coin, mark China's aerospace achievement over the past years. The commemorative note and coin will be circulated in the currency market with the same denomination as their equivalents. [Photo/Xinhua]
