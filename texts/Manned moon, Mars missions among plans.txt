Manned moon, Mars missions among plans

As planners for manned space missions move steadily toward the goal of placing Chinese astronauts on the moon, they have also started setting their sights on a much farther destination - Mars.Scientists have been making preparations for the country's manned lunar missions, including the establishment of a manned scientific outpost, according to the nation's leading space contractor, China Aerospace Science and Technology Corp.The company said the ultimate goal of China's lunar expeditions is to lay the scientific and technological foundations for manned missions to Mars.
