CPC publishes rectifications of inspected units

BEIJING -- All the 45 centrally-administrated enterprises and state institutions that had been examined during the third round of disciplinary inspections by the 19th Central Committee of the Communist Party of China (CPC) have made public their rectifications as of Sunday.

Details of the rectifications have been released on the website of the Central Commission for Discipline Inspection (CCDI) of the CPC and the National Supervisory Commission (NSC) from Friday to Sunday.

The third round of disciplinary inspection, carried out from March to June in 2019, covered 42 centrally-administrated enterprises, including China Aerospace Science and Technology Corporation, as well as three state departments in charge of various industries, including the State-owned Assets Supervision and Administration Commission of the State Council.

The Party committees of the inspected units have set up leading groups of inspection rectification, formulate rectification plans, clarify goals and tasks for rectification, and promote related work in strict accordance with the problems exposed in the inspections.

The latest round of inspections made the centrally-administrated enterprises the first sector that has been fully covered by the disciplinary inspections of the 19th CPC Central Committee.
