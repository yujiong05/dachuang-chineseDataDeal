标题: Luojia-1A satellite built to observe ground light

Luojia-1A satellite built to observe ground light

China has become most lighted nation in Asia, signaling its growth, expert saysChina will this year launch the country's first remote-sensing satellite dedicated to observing ground light at night, according to designers.The Luojia-1A, a 10-kilogram mini satellite, is being developed by scientists at Wuhan University in Hubei province and will carry a highly sensitive night light camera with a 100-meter ground image resolution, Li Deren, chief scientist of the project, told China News Service on Wednesday.
