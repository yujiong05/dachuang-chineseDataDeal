Satellite to monitor air, water

China launched the Gaofen 5 high-resolution Earth observation satellite on Wednesday to assist in the nation's environmental protection efforts.The satellite blasted off atop a Long March 4C carrier rocket at 2:28 am at Taiyuan Satellite Launch Center in Shanxi province. It was the 274th flight mission by a Long March rocket, according to the State Administration of Science, Technology and Industry for National Defense, which oversees the program.The satellite was developed and produced by the Shanghai Academy of Spaceflight Technology, a subsidiary of China Aerospace Science and Technology Corp, and will be used mainly by the Ministry of Ecology and Environment, the Ministry of Natural Resources and China Meteorological Administration.
